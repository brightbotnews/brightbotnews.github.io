About

Bright Bot News is a fun personal project to bring about absolutue hilarity. We summarize your favorite Alt-Right news in a digestable form inspired by Sumy the summarizer and tweetstorms.

I started this project based on @wearpants' suggestion over IRC. We bring to you nuggets of alternate wisdom from the erudites of our times.

This is, strictly speaking, the news through my lens and not a professional act of journalism, although, it is an act of journalism in and of itself. You won't find "fair and balanced" objective coverage. What you will find, however, is an independent voice, personal accountability, and a consideration for how the editorial choices I make impact others.

This project is hosted on GitHub Pages and the public GitHub repository can be found here. Log new issues, comments, feedback here.

Built using GitHub Pages, Jekyll, and Mailchimp.
